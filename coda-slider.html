Clemson's history. In style.

Clemson University is a special place for any one who has walked its grounds, cheered for its teams, learned in its classrooms. Since its inception there have been many events and people that have had a lasting impact on the University.

Chronicled on this timeline are the major historic events, little known tales and factoids of Clemson's history. In addition to the main timeline is a section displaying all of Clemson's presidents and what each has contributed.

How to order

The story of the timeline.

Having studied the University's history as seniors during a six-week course taught by Clemson's Historian, two students realized something:

While many alumni, students, faculty and anyone associated with Clemson hold it in such high esteem (and rightly so) few are aware of the wonderfully rich history that covers the campus.

Passionate about sharing their new found knowledge of Clemson's past, these two students set out to create a design that was both visually stunning and packed with facts. The result is the Clemson Timeline.
